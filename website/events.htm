Crazy Wisdom Bookstore Salon Evening
113 S. Main Street, Ann Arbor, Michigan
Thursday, May 5, 2011
7:00 pm

    An Evening with Psychoanalysts Kerry Kelly Novick and Jack Novick. Crazy Wisdom Salon Series.   Free.

    Nationally recognized psychoanalysts, the Novicks have written a new book, Emotional Muscle: Strong Parents, Strong Children. Locally, they are known for their innovative work in founding the Allen Creek Pre-School, which is a laboratory for integrating psychoanalytic insight with early childhood education. The Novicks will talk about their new book, and also field questions and comments.

    For more information, call 734-665-2757 or email bill@crazywisdom.net.
